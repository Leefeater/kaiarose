Animal Rights Activist

Animal rights is a big problem in today's society. Too many dogs, and cats are very mistreated by their owners, leaving them harmed, neglected or homeless. This doesn't get enough attention from the authorities, and it goes on unchecked.

Kaia is an animal lover. She always her parents know when one is around. She frequenly visits the under-priviliged animals at the shelter just to give them some love and attention. She has held events with other babies to present to them the importance of taking care of animals, as she believes that they have a right to a good life.
